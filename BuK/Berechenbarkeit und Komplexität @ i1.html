Information on homework and tutorials

If you want to take the exam, then you must
  • register for the exam at the ZPA.
  • register with our [homework system]
  • reach at least 50% of the points on the homework assignments.
About the organization of the instructions
  • Exercise sheets, exercise solutions and slides are provided via the L2P system. For joining the L2P room, you must first register for the course via RWTHOnline.
  • In case you have trouble in joining the L2P room, here is a direct link to the first few exercise sheets: [Uebung 1] [Uebung 2].
  • A new exercise sheet will be published every Friday (starting with Friday 19-Oct).
  • Homework (in groups of size two) is to be submitted at the box at i1 (Informatik-Zentrum, Erweiterungsbau E1, ground floor) every week by Wednesday, 12:15 o'clock.
    The first homework is due by Wednesday, 12:15 o'clock, 31-Oct.
  • For your homework scores and your assignment to tutorials (Kleingruppenübungen), we use the [system] designed by LuFGI2. Please register at that system, and announce your preferred tutorial times by Tuesday, 16.10, 23:55 o'clock. For further details, see [Uebung 0].
